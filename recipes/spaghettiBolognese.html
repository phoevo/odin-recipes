Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti bolognese consists of spaghetti with an Italian rag√π (meat sauce) made with minced beef, bacon and tomatoes, served with Parmesan cheese. Spaghetti bolognese is one of the most popular pasta dishes eaten outside of Italy.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ragu
  • Beef
  • Bacon
  • Tomatoes
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Firstly, fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add the uncooked spaghetti into the pot and let it boil for 6 minutes. As the it is boiling, start to heat up a sauce pan
  3. Add the raw minced beef into the sauce pan and your choice of powdered seasonings (salt, pepper, etc).
  4. Keep stirring the meat and the spaghetti so they don't stick to the bottom of the pot and pan.
  5. Chop your bacon into small squares and toss them into the pan with the cooking beef.
  6. Add your tomato sauce into to the pan with the beef and turn down the heat and let it simmer.
  7. As the spaghetti approaches its ideal cooked state, extract the spaghetti out of the water and add it into the pan with the beef and sauce.
  8. Keep stirring the pan with all the ingredients while waiting for it to simmer
  9. Onced the sauce has thickened and the spaghetti is nicely coated with it and the beef, turn of the heat and plate your food.
