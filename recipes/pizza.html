Pepperoni Pizza

Description

The pepperoni pizza is a simple pizza, yet one of the most popular choices. The pizza consists of a tomato based sauce, mozzarella cheese and slices of pepperoni sausage.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni Slices

Steps

  1. knead the dough into a pizza shape, with the circumfrence of the dough being thicker in order to create a nice crust
  2. Evently spread the tomato sauce on the dough
  3. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese evenly on the tomato sauce making sure to properly coat the perimeter of the sauce so there aren't large pockets of sauce on the finished pizza.
  4. Top the pizza with pepporoni slices and put it into the oven for 15-20 minutes at 180 degrees Celcius.
