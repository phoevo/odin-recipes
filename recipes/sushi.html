Sushi

Description

Sushi is a popular Japanese dish that consists of various types of raw fish, crab, squid combined with rice and sometimes vegetables depending on which style of sushi is being made. For this recipe we will focus on salmon maki roll sushi.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Sushi rice
  • Sushi seaweed
  • (Optional)Soy sauce
  • Wasabi

Steps

  1. Firstly, wash your sushi rice and place it into your rice cooker for 20 mins.
  2. Cut your salmon into thin strips, almost as long as your bamboo sheet
  3. Place and center your seaweed onto your bamboo sheet.
  4. When your rice is ready, get a scoop of rice and spread it out onto your seaweed, with the left and right sides almost touching the edge, while the top and bottom sides almost reach the edge of the seaweed.
  5. Place a salmon strip horizontally and towards the bottom edge of your bed of rice.
  6. Add a really thin strip of wasabi along the salmon
  7. Grab the ends of the bamboo sheet and start to wrap your ingredients.
  8. Tightly wrap your sushi roll, remove the bamboo sheet carefully and dice the role into your desired size.
  9. Use the soy sauce to dip your pieces of sushi before eating them.
